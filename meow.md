# What is a Cat?

A **cat** (*Felis catus*) is a small, domesticated carnivorous mammal. Here's the deal:

## Key Facts

- **Purposes:** Companionship, pest control (they're excellent hunters), and internet domination.
- **Sounds:** Meow (hello), purr (contentment), hiss (back off), chirp (excitement — usually at birds outside the window).
- **Sleep:** 12–16 hours per day. They are professional nappers.
- **Physics Defied:** Can always land on their feet (righting reflex). Also fit into any box, regardless of size.

## Famous Quote

> "Time spent with cats is never wasted." — Sigmund Freud (probably while being ignored by his cat).

## Cats in Tech

Cats are the unofficial mascots of the internet. Key examples:
- `cat` command in Unix — concatenates files (named for its ability to "con-cat-enate").
- LOLcats, Nyan Cat, Grumpy Cat — foundational internet culture.

A cat is essentially a tiny, furry roommate who judges you silently but secretly loves you. Probably.
